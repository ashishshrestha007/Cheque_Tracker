Frequently Asked Questions

To submit a new cheque, navigate to the "Cheque In" section from the sidebar. Fill in the required details such as cheque number, amount, payer information, and bank details. Upload an image of the cheque if required, then click "Submit" to process the cheque.
Standard cheque processing typically takes 2-3 business days. However, this may vary depending on the bank and the amount involved. You can track the status of your cheque in real-time through the dashboard or the Reports section.
Yes, you can cancel a pending deposit before it has been processed. Go to the Deposit section, find the pending transaction, and click on "Cancel" button. Note that once a deposit has been processed, it cannot be cancelled and you'll need to contact support for assistance.
Navigate to the Reports section from the sidebar. Select the type of report you want (daily, weekly, monthly, or custom date range). Choose the format (PDF, Excel, or CSV) and click "Generate Report". You can also schedule automatic report generation in the Settings.
If a cheque is rejected, you'll receive a notification with the reason for rejection. Common reasons include insufficient funds, signature mismatch, or expired cheque. Contact the issuing party to resolve the issue and submit a new cheque if necessary. For further assistance, contact our support team.
Go to Settings from the sidebar, then click on "Security". Under the "Change Password" section, enter your current password, then enter and confirm your new password. Click "Update Password" to save changes. Make sure to use a strong password with at least 8 characters.
Yes! ChequeTracker is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. You can access all features through your mobile browser. We're also working on dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android coming soon.
Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) for all data transmission. Your sensitive information is encrypted at rest, and we follow strict security protocols. We also offer two-factor authentication for additional account security. Regular security audits are performed to ensure your data remains safe.
